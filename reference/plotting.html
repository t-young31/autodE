For flat lists of r1, r2 and relative energies plot the PES by interpolating on a 50x50 grid after fitting with a 2d polynomial function

Parameters

  • r2 (np.ndarray) – r2 distance points

  • coeff_mat (np.array) – matrix of polynomial coefficients for the energy surface

Keyword Arguments

  • mep (list(tuple)) – list of coordinates on the grid for the min energy

  • across the surface (default (pathway) – {None})

  • name (str) – name of the plot (default: {‘2d_scan’})
